My Statement

Throughout this body of work I explored a multitude of ideas. Including those around America’s capitalist society, how music can immerse its listener, and my early experiences with the internet. Finally, as a resident in northern California, the impact of recent wildfires on the land and people who live in the surrounding areas.

This collection of videos and net art pieces were made over the past four months. For these works, I experimented with new forms of media and techniques. Filming and editing videos was a foreign task until tackling these projects, but I carried the same principles of dedication as I would with a familiar material. The net art websites have been made to test the viewer’s patients and navigation skills with only having one route and a definite end.
